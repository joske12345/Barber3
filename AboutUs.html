About Us

Welcome to Razor Sharp, a distinguished barbershop with roots tracing back to Blanchardstown. Steeped in tradition, our family-run business has been shaping the styles of the community for nearly a century. Recently, the torch has been passed to the youngest member of our family, infused with a passion to uphold the legacy, blend modern trends, and broaden our digital presence.
